Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

A faithful, streamlined take on the full croquet ruleset — complete with physics, power shots, and turn-based play for 2–4 players.

🏏

Physics-based shots

Tap and drag to aim. Drag distance sets power — the further you pull, the harder you swing. A mallet animation plays on every shot.

🎯

Full 16-passage course

Nine wickets, two stakes, and a proper sequence from start to finish — including the returning leg and centre-wicket double-traverse.

👥

2–4 players, one device

Pass-and-play multiplayer with blue, red, black, and yellow balls. Each player takes their turn on the same screen.

⚡

Bonus shots

Clear a wicket and keep going. Roquet an opponent's ball to earn croquet shots. Chain your way to a hat-trick.

🔈

Sound & haptics

Synthesised mallet cracks, wicket pings, and subtle haptic feedback on every hit. Toggle each independently in Settings.

🏆

Achievements

Seven unlockable achievements tracked locally and reported to Game Center — including the elusive Hat Trick.

How to Play

Up and playing in minutes

Croquet mirrors the real game. Navigate your ball through every wicket in sequence to win.

1

Choose your players

Pick 2 to 4 players. Blue always goes first, followed by red, black, and yellow.

2

Aim and swing

Tap and drag anywhere on screen. The drag direction becomes your aim and the drag distance sets power. Release to swing.

3

Clear wickets in order

Pass through each wicket in the correct direction and sequence. Clearing a wicket earns you a bonus shot — keep going!

4

Roquet your opponents

Hit another player's ball (a roquet) to earn two extra shots. Use the first to position their ball, then play a croquet shot.

5

Finish at the stake

The first ball to complete all 16 passages and hit the finishing stake wins. Hit the turning stake at the far end to start the return leg.

Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device

Croquet does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app works entirely offline — no internet connection is required or used. No analytics, no tracking, no ads.

The only data saved is your local game progress and settings, stored on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Game Center is optionally used for achievements. If enabled, achievement data is handled entirely by Apple under their privacy policy.
